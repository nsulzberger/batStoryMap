Murmeliwiese

On the Meadow Murmeliwiese, next to Bachtobelstrasse above Schweighofstrasse there is a good site so spot the bats living in the forest of the Üetliberg.

In the evening they leave the forest and come to the meadow grounds for hunting insects.

Its a bat site only known by the locals, that's why not so many bats have actually been recorded as you can see on the hotspot map.

Source: Ursula Tissot, FGZ AktionNaturReich
