Hardbrücke


At Hardbrücke, you can spot noctule bats hunting along the river Limmat and on the large meadow next to the school building.

Just after sunset is a good time to come here and watch bats, it should still be fairly bright.

When it’s darker you better walk along the river or lie on the meadow where you can spot common and Nathusiu’s pipistrelle.

(Source: Silvia Haug, Exkursionsführer Kt. Zürich)
