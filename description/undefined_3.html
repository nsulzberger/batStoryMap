Irchelpark

This site is great to enjoy a nice sunset, and afterwards, you can watch noctule bats hunting for insects.

Just sit at the border of the meadow and enjoy the view of flapping and sometimes clamouring bats.

A bit later, just at the border of the forest, you can spot common and nathusiu’s pipistrelle.

Source: Silvia Haug, Exkursionsführer Kt. Zürich
